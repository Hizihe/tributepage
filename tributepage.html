Chief Oberfemi Awolowo

The president we couldn't have

Here's a time line of Chief Obafemi Awolowo's life:

  • 1909- Born in Remo town ikenne
  • 1927- He enrolled at the University of London as an External student and Graduted with the degree of Bachelor of Commerce
  • 1944- He went to UK to study law at the University of London
  • 1946- He was call to Bar by the Honoroble Society of the inner Temple
  • 1949- Chief Obafemi Awolowo founded the Nigeria Tribune
  • 1952- Chief Obafemi Awolowo became the first Leader of Government Business and Minister of Local Government and Finance, and the fisrt Premier of the Western Region under Nigeria's parliametry system
  • 1954- Federal constitution was introduced in which the demand was led by Chief Obafemi Awolowo
  • 1959- Chief Obafemi Awolowo introduce primary education for all and free health care for children in Western Region and also establish the first television service in Africa and then the Oduduwa Group
  • 1959- He was the official Leader of the Opposition in the federal parliament to the Balewa government
  • 1963- He was imprisoned under the accusations of sedition and was not pardoned by the government until 1963
  • 1963- He assumed the role of Minister of Finance
  • 1979- He contested under the Unity party's platform as a presidential candidate but lost
  • 1983- He contested again under the same party and also lost
  • 1987- Chief Obafemi Awolowo Died at age 78 and was laid to rest
